# ZenSidebar Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

## Overview

ZenSidebar ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All data used by ZenSidebar is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

### 1. `tabs`
- **Purpose**: To list and manage your open browser tabs
- **Data Access**: Tab titles, URLs, and favicons
- **Storage**: Not stored; accessed in real-time only

### 2. `bookmarks`
- **Purpose**: To display and access your bookmarks in the sidebar
- **Data Access**: Bookmark titles, URLs, and folder structure
- **Storage**: Not stored; accessed in real-time only

### 3. `storage`
- **Purpose**: To save your preferences locally
- **Data Stored**: 
  - Floating ball position (x, y coordinates)
  - Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  - Sidebar state (open/closed)
- **Location**: Stored locally on your device only

### 4. `history`
- **Purpose**: To provide smart address bar suggestions
- **Data Access**: Your browsing history for autocomplete
- **Storage**: Not stored; accessed in real-time only

### 5. `favicon`
- **Purpose**: To display website icons next to tabs and bookmarks
- **Data Access**: Favicon URLs from websites
- **Storage**: Not stored; accessed in real-time only

### 6. `<all_urls>` (Host Permissions)
- **Purpose**: To inject the floating ball UI on all web pages
- **Data Access**: None; only used to display the UI
- **Storage**: No data is collected from web pages

## Data Storage

All user preferences are stored **locally** using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any external servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the Extension

## Third-Party Services

ZenSidebar does **NOT** use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted externally, your data security is managed by Chrome's built-in security features.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- View what data is stored locally (via Chrome DevTools)
- Delete all stored data (by uninstalling the Extension)
- Disable specific permissions (though this may affect functionality)
